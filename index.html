About

Twice Round Vintage is located on Haywood Road, in west Asheville, founded in 2001. Its a unique shop with lots of personality and exciting clothes, accessories, and vintage decor. We buy and sell quality vintage clothing ranging from the 1920s to the 1990s. New items in the shop weekly, come drop in and check it out!

If you have items to sell: please bring all clothing items folded or on hangers. Damaged or stained items cannot be accepted. Items that are out of season are also less likely to be accepted (don't bring puffer coats in June, etc.). Payment will be given by cash or check, we do not accept trades. Shoes, jewelry, belts, scarves, bags, and other accessories are welcome as well.
